      YouTube Channels I Recommend

      All free, all high quality. These are the channels that actually helped me understand concepts rather than just memorise commands.

      ▶

      TechWorld with Nana

      Best channel for Docker, Kubernetes, and full DevOps bootcamp. Nana explains everything step by step with real-world context.

      K8s · Docker · CI/CD
      ★ CKA & K8s pick
      ☸

      Piyush Sachdeva — 40 Days of CKA

      The best free CKA prep on YouTube. Piyush's "40 Days of CKA" series covers every exam domain hands-on — from cluster setup to troubleshooting — in a structured, day-by-day format that actually sticks.

      CKA · K8s · Exam Prep
      ▶

      NetworkChuck

      Makes Linux, networking, and DevOps concepts exciting and approachable. Great for beginners getting comfortable with the terminal.

      Linux · Networking · Cloud
      ▶

      Abhishek.Veeramalla

      Practical DevOps from an industry engineer. Real projects, real pipelines, and honest advice on what actually matters on the job.

      Jenkins · AWS · GitOps
      ▶

      Traversy Media

      Web fundamentals and deployment workflows. Useful when you need to understand what developers are building before you deploy it.

      Web · Deployment · Git
      ▶

      Fireship

      Lightning-fast concept breakdowns. "X in 100 seconds" videos are perfect for getting a mental model before diving deeper elsewhere.

      Concepts · Cloud · Tools
      ▶

      Anton Putra

      Deep dives into Kubernetes, Prometheus, Grafana, and GitOps. Excellent production-grade examples with full config files shared publicly.

      Observability · GitOps · EKS
      ▶

      That DevOps Guy

      Honest, practical DevOps content. Great for Helm, ArgoCD, GitOps patterns, and the real-world nuances that tutorials skip over.

      ArgoCD · Helm · GitOps
